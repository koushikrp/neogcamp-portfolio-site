An AI powered Open Source Photo Editor for refining precise details

August, 2021 * Studied about the research paper and explained my understanding with points from the paper.

Recent AI powered Photo Editors are making Photoshop redundant. You will be baffled by the Results Produced by these Computationally efficient Editors. One such Editor is SketchEdit: A Mask-Free Local Image Manipulator with Partial Sketches

Take a look at the Sketch-based image manipulation elaborated in the Github Repository : SketchEdit

Different from other existing methods used in popular photo editors, that usually require users to draw an extra mask indicating the region to modify, this new paradigm of sketch-based image manipulation only requires sketch inputs from users, utilizes the entire original image and gives the result..

One of the Outputs:

If we give an image and sketch, this Deep learning model automatically guesses the required target modification area and encodes it into a style vector which is formed in a structure independent way. Now the Generator creates the new content of the image based on the sketch and style vector. This manipulated image is finally produced by mixing the generator output into the modification area of the original image. This model is then trained in a supervised learning way by focusing the reconstruction of an image area from the sketch and style vector.

About Previous Approach

Before few months, another approach was discovered, called LaMa, that can be used to remove various objects from pictures like Trees, Animals, People, Houses,Signs, other miniature details etc. The network then regenerates the background behind the deleted objects and achieves excellent accuracy and performance even in challenging scenarios. Ex: Completion of periodic structures.
